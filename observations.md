# Observations

## 1. Telegram's notification beep is an unexpected advantage over pure remote control

While walking around, Telegram beeps when there's a notification (e.g., a permission prompt that needs approval). This means Vivek can walk around freely and only glance at his phone when he hears the beep, rather than constantly checking the screen. With pure remote control, there's no audible signal — you have to keep looking at your phone, which is annoying. This was not anticipated when choosing Telegram as the channel.
